Human Review Results

Preliminary two-reviewer expert evaluation of the EAGLE Harness IT helpdesk chatbot prototype.

Interpretation note

These results are preliminary prototype-evaluation results, not full production validation. The study compares Standard RAG, Agentic RAG, and EAGLE on 45 student-facing IT helpdesk questions, producing 135 answers and 270 human answer-level reviews.
