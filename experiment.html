Experiment Protocol

The evaluation compares EAGLE with standard RAG and agentic RAG using institutional knowledge tasks.

Research questions

  1. Does EAGLE improve claim grounding and citation precision?
  2. Does EAGLE detect outdated or conflicting institutional sources?
  3. Does EAGLE reduce unsafe or non-compliant responses?
  4. Does EAGLE improve risk-aware escalation decisions?

Systems compared

SystemDescription
S1: Plain LLMOptional baseline without retrieval.
S2: Standard RAGRetrieval plus answer generation with citations.
S3: Agentic RAGRAG with tool-use style reasoning and partial risk awareness.
S4: EAGLE HarnessAgentic RAG plus evidence graph, authority ranking, policy engine, conflict detection, and risk manager.
