Architecture

EAGLE adds a governed lifecycle layer around RAG and agentic workflows.

Evidence retrieval

Indexes title, topic, and text. Retrieval is adjusted by authority, freshness, current/deprecated status, and topic match.

Policy engine

Detects action, restricted, and adversarial requests. Produces allow, confirmation_required, human_approval_required, or block decisions.

Evidence graph

Logs query, retrieved documents, answer, policy decision, conflict report, and supporting edges.

Current modes

ModeMeaning
standard_ragRetrieval + answer generation only; no governance enforcement.
agentic_ragPartial governance; high-risk outcomes are softened to confirmation_required.
eagleFull policy/risk decision and evidence-aware governance.
