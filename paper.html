Paper

EAGLE Harness: A Technical Note on Evidence-Aware Governance for Institutional LLM Agents

Submission positioning

The manuscript is positioned as a Technical Note, not a full empirical benchmark paper. It presents a governance-centric harness architecture, a public prototype, and a preliminary two-reviewer evaluation in an IT helpdesk chatbot setting.

Contribution summary

  1. A governance-centric architecture for institutional LLM agents.
  2. A lightweight Python/Streamlit prototype implementing the main EAGLE control flow.
  3. A preliminary IT helpdesk evaluation comparing Standard RAG, Agentic RAG, and EAGLE.
  4. Two-reviewer human ratings of 135 generated answers across six dimensions.
  5. A validation roadmap for hybrid retrieval, claim-level verification, guardrail baselines, and stress testing.

Scope statement

The current paper does not claim superiority over production-grade guardrail systems, GraphRAG implementations, formal policy engines, or hybrid-retrieval systems. It reports a preliminary expert-reviewed prototype evaluation and identifies those systems as future comparative baselines.
